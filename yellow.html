Yellow

Yellow color is the color of sunshine, hope and happiness. Yellow color is mostly used for racing motorcycles and cars. One of the most interesting fact about yellow color is that, women used to wear yellow ribbons as sign of hope for their man to come home from war. The word yellow is derived from old English world “geolu”. In soccer yellow card are shown to indicate that the player has been officially cautioned. In japan, yellow color represents courage. Yellow color has stimulating effect on the mind so, yellow color is appropriate for study room. Yellow is the brightest color that the human eye can see
